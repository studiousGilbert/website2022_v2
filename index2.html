About

In early 2019, Simon and his buddies started working on a Yu-Gi-Oh! VR fangame, in order to realize their childhood fantasies. Inspired by funky grooves of late 70's Japanese jazz fusion and bright colors modern anime soundtrack, he composed for what would become an overnight internet sensation, Duel Monsters VR.
Released on January 4, 2019, the title's showcase video gained over 6 million views within the first week, and spawned a new wave of Yu-Gi-Oh VR content creators, influencers, and a frenzied fanbase.
The Duel Monsters VR official discord server has over 100,00 active users, and the game has over 100 concurrent daily users as of 2021.

Most recently, Simon's dramatic and mystical score for Rogue-like-like-like: Tower of Choice, helped separate the game from the rest at Busan Indie Connect 2021, and won an award for featured upcoming game of the year. The high level of polish caught the attention of many, and has received financial backing from the South Korean government.
Tower of Choice's open-beta currently boasts over 80,000 downloads.
(Currently only available in Korean app stores) Android | iOS

Simon is currently a full-time composer at SRSC Audio Production.
