About

In college, Simon and his buddies started working on a Yu-Gi-Oh! VR fangame, in order to realize their childhood fantasies. Inspired by Japanese jazz fusion and modern anime soundtrack, he composed for what would become an overnight internet sensation, Duel Monsters VR.
Released on January 4, 2019, the launch video gained over 6 million views within the first week, and spawned a new wave of Yu-Gi-Oh VR content creators, influencers, and frenzied fans.
The Duel Monsters VR official discord server has over 100,00 active users, and the game has over 100 concurrent daily users as of 2021.

Simon composed the score for Rogue-like-like-like: Tower of Choice, which was a featured game at Busan Indie Connect 2021. The high level of polish caught the attention of many, and has received financial backing from the South Korean government.
Tower of Choice's open-beta currently boasts over 80,000 downloads.
(Currently only available in Korean app stores) Android | iOS

Simon is currently a full-time composer at SRSC Audio Production. His work has been used in ads for Samsung, LG, Hyundai, and more.
